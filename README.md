# Neat Menu

Are you tired of mods cluttering up your menu and stacking button after button on the left side? Adding so many buttons that they overlap your beautiful Lethal Company logo? What about your mods that add menu icons not matching stylistically? Are you OCD like me and need more organization in your life? <br>

Introducing NeatMenu™, my one-click solution to all of your needs. With NeatMenu™, watch as all of your modded menu buttons are organized into a concise, colorful, and clean way. <br>

I've spent way more time modding Lethal Company than I ever thought I would. If you'd like to donate to help me make more content like this, I have a Ko-Fi here: [Ko-Fi link <3](http://ko-fi.com/nikkidarkmatter) <br>

*Currently supports the following mods:* MoreCompany, LethalPhones, LethalConfig, DawnLib (Achievements), and TwitchChatAPI. Also works alongside Emblem, although your mileage may vary depending on the theme you use. Other mod support may be added as requested! *Also features one Easter Egg button, but you'll have to install the mod to see that button. :3 Easter Egg button can be disabled in the config for the OCD.* <br>

*Currently requires MoreCompany. This may change in the future if requested by enough people. Other config options and customizations may be available in the future as well.* <br>
